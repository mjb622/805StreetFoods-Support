How We Can Help

805StreetFoods has different tools for different people: food truck fans, vendors, venues, and admins. Here are the most common things we help with:

For Food Truck Fans (App Users)

  • Questions about today's or upcoming truck schedules
  • Reporting incorrect event times, locations, or truck details
  • Issues with map, location permissions, or check-ins
  • Help with favorites, notifications, or badges
  • Questions about your account or sign-in (Apple, Google, email)

Account Access & Authentication

How to sign in:

  • Sign in with Apple (recommended)
  • Sign in with Google
  • Email/password accounts: Available by request only

OAuth-Only Authentication Model

805StreetFoods uses OAuth-only authentication for enhanced security. This means:

  • No self-service password accounts: Users cannot create email/password accounts themselves in the app
  • Request an account: If you prefer not to use Apple or Google sign-in, email admin@805streetfoods.com and we'll manually create an account for you
  • No password reset: We do not offer password reset functionality. This is an intentional security decision that eliminates password reset exploits and credential stuffing attacks
  • Locked out? If you can't access your Apple or Google account, contact those providers for account recovery. For email/password accounts created by our team, email us for assistance

Why OAuth-Only?

This approach provides several security benefits:

  • Eliminates password reset attack vectors
  • Prevents credential stuffing and password reuse attacks
  • Leverages Apple and Google's industry-leading security infrastructure
  • Automatic two-factor authentication support through OAuth providers
  • Breach monitoring and account protection managed by your OAuth provider

For Vendors & Venues

  • Requesting vendor access for your food truck or venue
  • Help editing truck or venue profiles (logos, photos, links, dietary tags)
  • Scheduling, editing, or canceling events
  • Understanding Basic vs. Pro vendor features
  • Questions about featured placement and visibility

Subscriptions & Billing (Vendors)

Vendor subscriptions and featured placement are managed on our external vendor site, not through Apple’s in-app purchases.

  • Questions about your current subscription tier (Free, Basic, Pro)
  • Issues with access to Pro features (photos, notifications, status updates)
  • Featured boost visibility or expiration questions
  • Billing or subscription changes made on the vendor website

If you email us about billing, please include:

  • The email address you use to sign in
  • Your truck or venue name(s)
  • A short description of what changed or what you expected to see

Account & Data Requests

You can always contact us about your account or your data at admin@805streetfoods.com.

  • Help accessing your account if you’re locked out
  • Updating your profile information if you can’t do it in the app
  • Requesting a copy of your data (favorites, check-ins, ratings, etc.)
  • Requesting account deletion and data removal where possible

Reporting a Bug or Issue

When something isn’t working, details really help us fix it quickly. If you can, please include in your email:

  • What you were trying to do (for example: “check in”, “favorite a truck”, “edit an event”)
  • What happened instead (error message, blank screen, crash, wrong data, etc.)
  • Your device model and iOS version (if you know it)
  • Approximate date/time the problem occurred
  • Screenshots if you’re comfortable sharing them
